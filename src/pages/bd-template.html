P&G JOLT CMS DESIGN

Previous processes for content creation and the usage of previous CMSs were not efficient or intuitive for those involved. P&G wanted to retain the use of Sitecore, but transform it to fit the needs of a variety of users.
  • Main Goal: To improve and champion the content authoring side of the JOLT solution and deliver functionality to support P&G’s Global Content Creation process for the everydayme.com brand (a.k.a. the Mastersite). Our deliverables include:
    • The Image Cropping Tool
    • Experience Editor authoring improvements
    • Custom Launchpad Capabilities to create or edit pages without having to navigate through Sitecore’s Content Editor
    • The Mastersite, including:
      1. Viewing and searching for global JOLT editorial content
      2. Sharing Editorials between JOLT locales via the Mastersite
      3. Viewing analytics performance of live JOLTeditorial pages
      4. Transperfect Integration
      5. Workflow and Security
  • Current State of the Project: We are coming up on our final weeks of the project to close our remaining work and perform knowledge transfer to the Proximity team.
  • Timeline: May 2017 - December 15, 2017. JOLT UK Site Beta Launch is planned for November 20.
  • Who is on the project team? Artem, Marijana, Maksim, Marko, Mike, Lanesa, Katya, Ken, and Katie.
